Hướng dẫn sử dụng màn hình Sản phẩm.

Thêm mới sản phẩm

Bước 1: To change the way a picture fits in your document, click it and a button for layout options appears next to it. When you work on a table, click where you want to add a row or a column, and then click the plus sign. Reading is easier, too, in the new Reading view. You can collapse parts of the document and focus on the text you want.
